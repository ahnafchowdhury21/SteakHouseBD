About Steak House

Steak house is a recently opened restaurant in Bangladesh that has gained a lot of popularity in under 2 months which is a very big achievement for us. Our CEO of this company is "Ahnaf Chowdhury". He is a software engineer and he works at Microsoft as a senior programmer. He was thinking of starting a business and Walaah! He opened a restaurant. In this shop we want you to sit in your chair and enjoy the most beautiful scenerio of Gulshan from the tallest Building with our deeply fried with love steaks. Here you will find all type Halal steaks that exists in the planet.

We always make sure that our customers are happy with what service we provide or our food and so we cook the steaks as greatly as humanly possible.

Thank You for being with us. If you are new to the restaurant, make sure to share our website, facebook and instagram page so that they can also enjoy the beauty of our restaurant and our steaks.
